About ESL Job Map

Find Jobs Near You!

Zoom into your neighborhood to look for jobs near you. We even have city-level zoom shortcuts in the navigation bar for Seoul and Busan!

Old Jobs Are Removed!

Part-time and full-time jobs expire after 30 days. Those who post the jobs can also take them down earlier if they have found a candidate! This keeps our job postings fresh.

Apply Quickly!

Apply without having to register on our site; or make an account and apply in just two clicks! When you register, we can save your resume and application information, so that it can be automatically filled in and attached.

Stop Seeing Jobs You Aren’t Interested In!

If you apply while signed in, the icon will turn black. If you decided you are Not Interested in a job, click that text and the icon will be grayed out. This will mean that when looking in your area, only new jobs you haven’t seen will be stand out.

Keep up with us or ask questions on our Facebook page.

Posting Jobs

Posting Jobs

You can post jobs, after you register or login. You can then track and close the jobs you have posted at any time. You can see the applications to your jobs on our site, and you will also receive an email copy of the application whenever someone applies.
